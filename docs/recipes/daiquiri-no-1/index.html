Daiquiri No. 1

Coupe Rum

The most elegant, simple, and famous of all Cuban cocktails. The Daiquiri and its variants were set in stone by legendary barman Constantino Ribalaigua of the famous Florida bar in Havana.

rum classic daiquiri cuban

Ingredients

  • lightly aged rum (2 oz)
  • fresh lime juice (3/4 oz)
  • SC Demerara Syrup (1/2 oz)

Instructions

  1. Add all the ingredients to a cocktail shaker{}. Add lightly aged rum, fresh lime juice, and SC Demerara Syrup.
  2. Shake, and then double-strain cracked or cubed ice, shake, and then double-strain into a chilled coupe.

Recipe by Bar La Florida Cocktails, 1935 reprint, adapted by Smuggler's Cove
