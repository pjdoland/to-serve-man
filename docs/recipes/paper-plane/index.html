Coupe Bourbon Easy

A perfectly balanced modern classic—equal parts everything, impossibly good.

bourbon bitter stirred modern-classic

Ingredients

  • bourbon (3/4 oz)
  • Aperol (3/4 oz)
  • Amaro Nonino (3/4 oz)
  • lemon juice (3/4 oz, fresh)

Instructions

  1. Add bourbon, Aperol, Amaro Nonino, and lemon juice to a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds until well chilled.
  3. Double strain through a fine mesh strainer into a chilled coupe glass.
  4. No garnish needed—the drink speaks for itself.
