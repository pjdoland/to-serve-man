Saturn

Coupe Gin

A winning combination of gin, lemon, passion fruit, falernum, and orgeat. Created for the 1967 World Championship, this drink represents the rings of Saturn.

gin tiki falernum passion-fruit

Ingredients

  • London dry gin (1 1/4 oz)
  • fresh lemon juice (3/4 oz)
  • passion fruit syrup (1/2 oz)
  • orgeat (1/2 oz)
  • John D. Taylor's Velvet Falernum (1/4 oz)
  • lemon peel (wrapped into rings)

Instructions

  1. Add London dry gin, fresh lemon juice, passion fruit syrup, orgeat, and John D. Taylor's Velvet Falernum to a cocktail shaker{} filled with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled coupe.
  3. Garnish with lemon peel to represent the rings of Saturn. Rest garnish on top of the drink.

Recipe by J. "Popo" Galsini, adapted by Smuggler's Cove
